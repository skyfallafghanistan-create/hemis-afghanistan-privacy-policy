Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

Hemis Afghanistan (we) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application, Hemis Afghanistan, handles user information.

Purpose of the App

Hemis Afghanistan is designed to help university students in Afghanistan view their academic information, such as test results, through official university systems.

Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app does not create user accounts and does not store personal information on our servers.

Use of Information

Any information displayed in the app is retrieved temporarily for viewing purposes only and is not saved, logged, or processed by us.

Third-Party Services

Hemis Afghanistan does not use third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or data collection SDKs.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store user data, there is no personal data retained by the app that could be exposed or misused.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for university students and is not designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us via the support information provided in the Google Play Store listing.
